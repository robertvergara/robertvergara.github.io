Help Robert Survive Cancer

Robert Vergara is fighting for his life and needs your help.

"Spending his time as the charismatic face of the Gateway for the past five years, along with pursuing his passion for the arts as a visual artist and art educator, Robert is a friend to many."

Updates

Following his recent diagnosis of Metastasized Papillary Thyroid Cancer at 33 years old, Robert's surgery, to remove the thyroid gland and lymph nodes on the right side of his neck, has been scheduled forMay 1st at SF General. Post-surgery treatment will begin in the form of radiated iodine.

We have updated the website! There are three new sections: the button "Blog" has been added. This is a personal space where Robert can share his thoughts and feelings throughout this experience. Another button "Surgery Details" has been added to post updates regarding the surgery. Lastly, the button "Help" was added which features a calendar where the generous donation of one's time can be scheduled, to assist Robert with rides to and from Dr. appointments, etc.

At the end of July, an art event will be hosted as a fundraiser. All proceeds will go towards Robert's surgery and treatment. Details to follow!!!

Help out or Donate

We want Robert’s energy to be focused fully on his recovery which means that any support we can give that will alleviate his financial and logistical worries is critical.

We hope that you will continue to help us build a network of support for Robert and keep him in your thoughts through this difficult time. Any contribution you can make—in either time or money—will be a lifeline to Robert as he undergoes what will, no doubt, be a difficult and costly journey back to health.
